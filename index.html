About me

I am an undergraduate student of Data Science, Fudan University. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Meiyue Shao. Before joining the School of Data Science, I spent a year as a Natural Science student, where I made up my mind to devote myself to the elegance of artificial intelligence and mathematics. I have been proud of being an alumni of Shanghai Experimental School, where I spent a wonderful six-year with a group of like-minded genius.

My primary research interest currently focuses on numerical algebra. Meanwhile, I am also seeking to delve deeper into other cross-disciplinary topics related to reinforcement learning and theory. I am planning to be an exchange student to Nanyang Technological University in August, 2022, and hopefully I might get involved in some research programs. Being a productive researcher is all that I dreamt of about my future career. And the world of mystery is really fascinating!!
